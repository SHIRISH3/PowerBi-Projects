📈 **Key Insights Delivered:**

* **Batting Performance:** Comprehensive view of player span, matches and innings played, total runs, highest score, strike rate, batting average, balls faced, not-outs, boundary distribution (4s & 6s), and milestone achievements including half-centuries, centuries, and ducks.
* **Bowling Performance:** Detailed analysis covering wickets taken, bowling strike rate, runs conceded, overs and maiden overs bowled, economy rate, best bowling figures, bowling average, and notable performances such as four- and five-wicket hauls.
* **Fielding Performance:** Evaluation of fielding contributions including stumpings, catches (as wicketkeeper and fielder), total dismissals, dismissal-per-innings (D/I) ratio, match involvement, and overall defensive impact on the game.
